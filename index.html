Biography

Yuxin LI (Ryan) is currently biocomputation researcher at Baidu Biocomputation Lab. He obtained his Master of Science degree in Computer Science at the Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2023. He obtained his Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science at the Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2021. In his preultimate year, Ryan went on exchange in Nanyang Technological University in Singapore as a one-semester exchange student in Computer Science. He joined Tencent in 2021 as a Game Engineer.

His research interests include Deep Generative Models, AI + Healthcare, Protein Design
